The pieces of an RL problem

👁️ Observation

What the agent perceives each step. Here, the 3×3 window around you — not the full board. Two very different spots can look identical through this window.

🎮 Action

The choices available: move up, down, left, or right. The agent picks one action every step.

🏆 Reward

The score signal: +10 for a gem, −10 for a spike. The agent's whole goal is to collect as much reward as possible.

🧭 Policy

The strategy that turns an observation into an action. You were a policy while you played. Next we'll train one from your moves.

Learning from you

Now train agents from how you played

Every move you made was an (observation → action) example, and every gem or spike was a reward. We'll turn your run into two different learners:

Copycat (imitation): learns to repeat the action you took for each thing you saw.
Optimizer (reward RL): ignores your style and learns to chase the most reward.
